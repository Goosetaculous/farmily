What We Do?

From spectacular beaches to golden sunny weather, San Diego is also home to more than 6,500 farms. In fact, we have more small farms than any other county in the nation, contributing to a $1.5 billion dollar agriculture industry.

Farmily helps home farmers maximize their vital online exposure and presents to you, your personal farm to fork experience.
